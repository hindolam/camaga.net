Registration

Update as of Jan 30, 2015 - Due to overwhelming response, camaga is unable to accept any new registration

  1. Registration form
    If you are interested in participating in the Great Composers Day 2015, download the following registration form.

    2015 Composers' Day Registration Form  

    The registration form is required from

    • Individual performers
    • Group participants
    • Accompanists (Pakkavadyams), and
    • individuals interested in participating only in the group singing of Pancharathna Krithis and Utsava Sampradaya kritis

    Attach the completed Registration Form to your email and send it to camaga.atlanta@gmail.com
  2. Group Participants Registration
    However, if you are intending to participate as a member of a teacher’s group, only your teacher should submit the form on behalf of the group. We do not accept registration form received directly from a member of a group or from parents or from other participants in a group.
  3. Note for Teachers
    In the case of your student groups, kindly include the names of each member of the group, and denote the group (by a group number) to which each person belongs. Kindly note that CAMAGA does not decide the groups or its members; it must be decided only by the respective teachers.
    In the case of groups, we request the teachers to collect the registration fees from each non-CAMAGA member of their groups and mail the check directly to CAMAGA (6011 Rocky Shoals Court, Tucker, GA, 30084).
  4. Registration fee for non-members
    Registration fee is required of participants who are not members of CAMAGA. Each participant who is a not a member of CAMAGA in 2015 must pay a registration fee of $25 (whether participating in a group, as an individual, and performing as an accompanist).

    Individual
    $25
  5. CAMAGA members’ registration
    Members should have renewed their CAMAGA membership for the calendar year 2015 by January 31st, 2015. If otherwise, they should send in a registration fee of $ 25 per participant.

    Family
    $200
    Individual
    $100
    Student with Id
    $50

    Membership form   

  6. Receipt of registration fees (for all participants)
    Kindly note that the registration forms and the registration fee from non-members or CAMAGA membership fee is received by CAMAGA latest by January 31, 2015.
  7. Registration acknowledgement
    Once we receive the registration email and the registration form, within 72 hours, we will send you an acknowledgement confirming receipt of your registration. It is your responsibility to ensure that you received an acknowledgement. If you did not receive an acknowledgement within five days of sending in your registration, you must contact CAMAGA and get the matter resolved. After the lapse of five days, we will not accept any further requests to include your registration.
  8. Early closure of registration
    If we receive more requests than we can accommodate within the two-day function, CAMAGA will close the registration process earlier than January 31, 2015.
  9. Note
    No changes to assigned slot will be made after the program has been announced.
  10. Participants can choose to sing the compositions of pre-Trinity, Trinity, and post-Trinity compositions.
