Objective Science or Subjective Spirituality?

In conformity with these objectives, science has contributed immensely to our understanding of the material worldandto improving our living conditions, including fighting poverty and hunger in the world. But, sciencealso has contributed to misery and destruction in the world. Inventions such as chemical and biological weapons have made the world a less safe place to live.
